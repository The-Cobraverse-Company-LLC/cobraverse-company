The four bloodlines

Choose your cobra

Every faction has its own powers, worlds, and stories. Pick a side.

Laser Cobras

Origin unknown. Threat level: absolute.

No one knows what world birthed the Laser Cobras — only that they arrived, and everything changed. These apex predators don't hunt so much as erase, their signature eye-lasers cutting the air at light speed before a target even registers movement. Territory doesn't stay contested for long once a Laser Cobra sets its sights on it — resistance folds fast, and what's left rarely puts up a second fight. To face one is to already be behind.

Element · Light

Mecha Cobras

Built for war. Built by someone.

Cold, precise, and devastatingly efficient, the Mecha Cobras are machines of pure combat purpose — every servo and blade honed for one function: destruction. Their true origin is still debated, but the evidence points to the stars. They struck Earth first, without warning or explanation, then turned their assault toward Cobraxis itself. Whether they're weapons, scouts, or something worse, one truth holds: whoever built them wanted something dead.

Element · Steel

Ice Cobras

Silent until it's too late.

Across the frozen wastes of Cobraxis, the Ice Cobras have mastered the art of disappearing into the landscape itself. They don't announce their presence — they simply arrive by stealth to infiltrate your defenses. By the time their ice daggers form and their shield-walls rise, the fight is already on their terms. Patient, precise, and nearly invisible until the moment they choose not to be.

Element · Frost

Fire Cobras

Born from the mountain's rage.

Forged in the molten rivers that scar the surface of Cobraxis, the Fire Cobras carry the volcano's fury in their scales. They don't strike so much as detonate — flame and force overwhelming most who stand against them before a defense can even form. Enemies that survive the first wave rarely survive the second; the Fire Cobras don't relent once the burning starts, and few adversaries walk away from the aftermath.

Element · Flame
