Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Cobraverse Company LLC ("Cobraverse," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit cobraverse.net and our related sites and services (the "Services"), including any mobile applications we publish. By using the Services, you agree to this Policy.

1. Who we are

The Cobraverse Company LLC is a media studio that produces video games, videos, and merchandise. You can contact us any time through our contact form.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

When you submit our contact form, we collect the name, email address, and message you provide so we can respond to you. We only use this information to reply to your inquiry.

Information collected automatically

We use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics tool, to understand general site usage (such as page views, referring sites, browser type, and approximate country). GoatCounter does not use tracking cookies and does not collect personally identifying information. See the GoatCounter privacy statement.

3. Third-party service providers

We rely on a small number of service providers to operate the Services:

  • Web3Forms — processes and delivers contact-form submissions to our email. See the Web3Forms Privacy Policy.
  • hCaptcha — protects our forms from spam and abuse and may collect device and usage data to tell humans from bots. See the hCaptcha Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website and may process standard server logs (such as IP addresses) to deliver the site.
  • YouTube — some pages may embed videos; YouTube may set cookies and collect data under Google's policies.

4. Cookies

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Some third-party features above (for example, hCaptcha or embedded YouTube videos) may set their own cookies when you interact with them, governed by their respective policies.

5. How we use information

  • To respond to your messages and provide customer support.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse or abuse.

6. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the service providers listed above as needed to operate the Services, or when required by law.

7. Data retention

We keep contact-form messages for as long as needed to respond to you and maintain our records, and then delete them when no longer required.

8. Children's privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided to us through our contact form. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

11. Contact us

Questions about this Policy? Reach us through our contact form.
