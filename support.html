Help & Support

Support

The Cobraverse Company LLC

Need help with one of our games, apps, videos, or merchandise orders? We're here to help. The fastest way to reach us is our contact form — it goes straight to our support team.

Contact our support team

Use our contact form to send us a message. Please include as much detail as you can — the product or app name, your device, and a description of the issue — so we can help you faster.

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

Apps & games

For issues with one of our applications — crashes, bugs, purchases, or feature questions — contact us through the form above and include your device model and operating system version. This helps us reproduce and fix the problem quickly.

Merchandise & orders

Apparel and physical merchandise for our Laser Cobras brand are sold through our Etsy store.
